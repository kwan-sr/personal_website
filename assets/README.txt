ASSETS FOLDER
=============

Drop your files here and the site will pick them up automatically:

  headshot.jpg     - Your profile photo (square works best, 400x400+)
  cv.pdf           - Your CV as a PDF (linked from the Contact card)
  art/             - Folder for artwork images (art1.jpg, art2.jpg, etc.)

If headshot.jpg is missing, the site falls back to your initials ("KS") as a placeholder.
